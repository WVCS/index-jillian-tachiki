    ABOUT

    SubZero has been hosting their annual parties since 2014. We transformed an old farm into a location where you can party all night. Every year, the event expands to be able to accomodate all of the new activites we add. We've become the one stop shop for all the winter activites you want to do. This year we have almost 50 acres of fun! That means that there's sure to be something for you, no matter who you are. SubZero is perfect for families, friends, couples, and anybody else who just wants to have a great time. Party the whole night away at SubZero!
