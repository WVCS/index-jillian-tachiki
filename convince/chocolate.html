    • White Chocolate is Bad

    1. It's not even chocolate.


    White chocolate contains no cacao solids, so technically it doesn’t even qualify as chocolate. It’s only sugar, milk, and cocoa butter. What’s the point?

    2. It’s more unhealthy than milk or dark chocolate.


    No chocolate can really be considered healthy, but the cacao in milk, and especially in dark chocolate gives them antioxidants that white chocolate doesn’t have.

    Which Type of Chocolate is the Healthiest, and Why?

    3. It tastes bad.


    It's solid, sweet milk. There's no complexity in it's flavor; only one note of plain sweet.

    Save your body and your taste buds. Don't eat white chocolate.
