The Simulation

It has all fallen apart. Everything you thought you knew has been a lie. Everyone has been being controlled without even knowing it. You and your team have figured out a way to shut down the operation, but you'll be tight on time. If you fail, you'll surely be put back into the simulation with no chance of escape, or worse. Breach the security of those in charge and destroy the simulation, or forever live in this lie.
