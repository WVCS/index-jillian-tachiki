Mission to Mars

You and your team were chosen to be the first people to go to Mars. During your journey, somehow your ship veered off course, and now the whole mission could be in jeopardy. Ground control estimates that you only have about an hour to get back on course before you loose connection with them and are never heard from again. Figure out what's wrong by unlocking the various interworkings of your ship and fix the problem before it's too late.
