Enchanted

Enchanted

You and your friends woke up in a cottage in the middle of an enchanted forest. The enchantress that has captured you warns that she has put a spell on you. You have one hour to escape or you'll be stuck in this world forever! Navigate through the cottage and solve puzzles together in order to gather the three artifacts you need in order to get out and return home.
