Breakout

You and your fellow inmates were framed for a murder you didn't commit. Luckily, it seems that the last prisoners in your cell started a plan to escape that you can continue. You don't have much time before the guards return and figure out your plan. Enact the perfect escape before it's too late and you're stuck here for who knows how long. Maybe, if you're lucky, you'll even get to find out who actually commited the crime and get your revenge.
