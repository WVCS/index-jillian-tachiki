FAQ

What do we get if we escape?

While there is no physical prize, we do encourage you to take a picture afterward so that you can brag to your friends.

Where should we park?

There is a lot down the street to the left of the building, about a two minute walk, or paid parking right next to the complex.

How many people can play?

The number of participants depends on the room, with each acomadating somewhere between 2-6 players.

Do I need any prior puzzle knowledge?

Although prior knowledge will help you be more effecient, no knowledge about puzzles or locks is required. All of this will be explained during the debrief before you enter the room.

Why should I come to Crypt?

Crypt offers six unique and challenging puzzles that you're sure to enjoy no matter how experienced you are!
